Prime Rib

This recipe will help you make the best prime rib of your life!

Ingredients

  • Prime rib
  • parrila salt
  • black pepper
  • Olive oil

How to cook

  1. Add the ingredients to the Prime rib peace
  2. Turn the oven on at 200 degrees
  3. Cook it on a grill, 4 minutes each side
  4. Cook it on the oven for 7 minutes each side
